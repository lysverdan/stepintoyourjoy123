Intuitive Consultations

Connect with wisdom, clarity, and divine guidance

During an Intuitive Consult, the light beings that I partner with share their wisdom and energy with you. This wisdom is intended as guidance to your truest wisest self. Their information is shared gently and the sessions offer you peace and clarity.

The messages though are only one part of the sessions. As you ask your questions, and receive answers the energy from these pure loving energies also flows through their words into your energy fields clearing and realigning you to your unique vibrational imprint once again.

The energy that flows through stays with you long after the session is complete. It supports you in shifting into new ways of seeing, believing and being.

The results are as unique as you are... though everyone shares some similar experiences.

You will feel clear, uplifted and inspired with a deeper connection to yourself and the world. Many people also share that they feel more empowered and confident in their lives. And if you and your loved ones who have passed on are ready, a connection can often be made during your session.
